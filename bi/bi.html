Answer Writing Tips for Business Intelligence

  • Start with clear definitions for fundamental concepts
  • Use diagrams for architectures (BI Infrastructure, Data Warehouse)
  • Include specific examples for each concept
  • Compare and contrast when asked (e.g., OLAP vs OLTP)
  • Mention real-world applications and case studies
  • For process questions, use step-by-step explanations

Key Comparisons to Memorize

Comparison Key Differences
BI vs Business Analytics BI: Descriptive, what happened? BA: Predictive, what will happen?
OLAP vs OLTP OLAP: Analytical, read-heavy, historical. OLTP: Transactional, write-heavy, current
Data Warehouse vs Data Mart DW: Enterprise-wide, integrated. DM: Department-specific, focused
Star vs Snowflake Schema Star: Denormalized, simple. Snowflake: Normalized, complex
Structured vs Unstructured Data Structured: Tabular, schema-defined. Unstructured: Text, images, no schema
